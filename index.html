Therapy Services

Smile Achievers was founded on a simple belief: every child deserves support that feels encouraging, respectful, and genuinely joyful. We partner closely with parents to build skills in ways that are practical for home, school, and everyday life.

Based in Ingleburn, our clinic is proud to serve local families with thoughtful care, clear communication, and personalised plans that help children grow with confidence. Creating Smiles, Inspiring Lives is more than a tagline — it is how we show up each day.

Our Services

What We Offer

Occupational Therapy

We help children build independence in daily routines through practical, engaging therapy. Sessions support fine motor development for tasks like writing, dressing, and self-care. We also focus on school readiness so children feel capable and confident in learning environments.

Fine MotorSelf-CareSchool Readiness

Sensory Integration

Our sensory integration therapy supports children who experience the world more intensely or differently. We design sensory diets that promote regulation across home, school, and community settings. Through play-based strategies, children learn to feel calmer, more organised, and ready to participate.

Sensory DietRegulationPlay-Based

Speech Therapy

We support language development and articulation in ways that are positive and child-led. Sessions can include AAC support so every child has a strong voice and meaningful ways to communicate. Our goal is to grow communication confidence for connection, learning, and everyday expression.

LanguageArticulationAAC
